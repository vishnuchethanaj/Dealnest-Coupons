# Dealnest-Coupons
Digital coupon systems and hyperlocal marketplaces have transformed consumer purchasing behavior over the past decade. The integration of mobile applications, location-based services, and digital payments has enabled businesses to offer targeted promotional deals to attract customers. 
Several global and Indian platforms have implemented various coupon distribution and discount models. 
This literature survey examines existing systems, their methodologies, strengths, and limitations, and identifies the research gap addressed by the proposed DealNest platform.
